VOICES OF LA CROSSE

Related to Footsteps of La Crosse, these maps were created to accompany the Hear, Here public histories project in La Crosse Wisconsin. The Hear, Here project placed plaques around La Crosse, Wisconsin with a phone number and extension, and when called, played recorded audio of a person's experience of that place. These maps group thematically related plaques to create coherent walking tours.

The maps can be found here.
