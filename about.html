Hello, welcome! I'm a mapmaker, fiction writer, and a lover of strange animals, board games, and all things fantastical. Currently, I'm a graduate student at the University of Wisconsin, studying Cartography. My research interests include intersections between atlases, storytelling, open-source cartography, the digital humanities, and using maps to examine and challenge power. Globes are another interest of mine, and I endevour to make my own (it's very hard, so please reach out if you construction tips/techniques). Check out my resume if you think our interests might align, and I'd love to hear from you!
